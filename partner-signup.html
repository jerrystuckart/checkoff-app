Questions

What exactly is "an item on the list"?
We write a short, compelling experience description tied to your business — something like "Try the off-menu secret burger at Joe's" or "Order the house old fashioned at the bar with no sign." It appears in the CheckOff app for every user in your neighborhood and shows up in their Nearby feed and curated lists.
What's a "secret reveal"?
A secret item shows as "🔒 Secret item" in the app until the user's GPS confirms they're physically at your location. Then it unlocks and reveals a special experience — like a hidden menu item, a discount, or an insider tip. It's a powerful reason to make the trip.
How does ring placement work?
CheckOff sorts nearby items into rings: Core (your neighborhood), Near (easy drive), Metro (worth the trip), and Destination. Higher tiers get placed in outer rings so more people across the metro area see your item, not just your immediate neighbors.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly plans cancel with no penalty. Annual plans are non-refundable after 30 days, but we'll honor the term through your paid period.
How long until my item is live?
We'll reach out within 1 business day to confirm your item details and get your GPS location. Most items go live within 48 hours.
